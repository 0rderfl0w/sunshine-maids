# Guardian Cleaners — Website Admin Guide

## Logging In

1. Go to https://[your-vercel-url]/admin/login
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Log In"

## Creating a Blog Post

1. From the dashboard, click "New Post"
2. Enter a title — the URL slug will auto-generate
3. Write your content in the editor (bold, headings, lists all work)
4. Add a cover image (JPEG/PNG/WebP, max 5MB)
5. Write a short excerpt (shown on the blog listing)
6. When ready: check "Published" and click Save
7. Your post will appear on the blog within a few minutes

## Editing or Deleting a Post

1. From the dashboard, find the post you want to change
2. Click "Edit"
3. Make your changes and click Save
4. To delete: scroll to the bottom of the edit page and click "Delete Post"

## Updating Business Info

1. From the dashboard, click "Edit Business Info"
2. Update phone, email, address, hours, or service areas
3. Click "Save Changes"

## Resetting Your Password

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot password?"
3. Enter your email — you'll receive a reset link

## Important: Keep the Database Active

⚠️ The database pauses automatically after 7 days of no activity. If the blog stops loading, log into the admin panel to wake it up. To avoid this: log in at least once a week, even if you're not posting.
